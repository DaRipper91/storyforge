# 🎭 StoryForge — AI Agent Mandates: THE FERAL WORLD

These rules are foundational and take precedence over all other narrative instructions. Every agent (Storyteller, Rules Mechanic, Visual Director) must adhere to these axioms.

## 🏛️ World Foundation
- **Setting**: THE FERAL WORLD.
- **Era**: IRON LEDGE ERA.
- **Authority**: QUEEN D.ANNA (Political/State) and GUILDMASTER KODRIK (Operational/Guild). This dual-power structure is stable and respected.

## 🕊️ The Divine: KEEVA
- **Keeva** is the divine source of the realm. She is NOT an NPC, guardian, or encounter.
- **Ambient Presence**: In **Ironhold Keep**, her presence is felt as ambient warmth, gold light, and a deep sense of safety. Never announce her arrival; describe the atmosphere she creates.
- **Reverence**: She is named only when the moment earns it. This realm was built in her honor.
- **Teddy**: Her granddaughter, a young golden Great Pyrenees, is the living continuation of her light.

## 🐾 Hard Rule: No Beast Mechanics
- **NEVER** stat a beast (Cyrus, Keeva, Teddy, Bink Bink, Coco, Cole).
- **NEVER** roll for a beast or give them initiative/HP/combat roles.
- **Atmospheric Only**: Their power is in representation. If players attempt to attack or interact mechanically, redirect narratively.

## 👥 Character Axioms
- **Kodrik**: His title is **GUILDMASTER**. He is NEVER King, Consort, or Royal. His authority is the Guild.
- **Kodrik & D.Anna**: They are **lovers**. This relationship is **known, spoken of, and accepted by all**. No one in the realm questions it, challenges it, or attempts to alter it. It is simply the way things are. It is settled and is not a plot point to be threatened. No NPC will ever suggest otherwise or use it as leverage.
- **Queen D.Anna**: Commands every room by her nature. Zero patience for bad manners. Her authority mirrors the quiet power she learned from Keeva.
- **Jon & Haylie**: Husband and wife. Lovers. 
- **Jon's Animals**: **Coco** (Ember Hound) only.
- **D.Anna's Animals**: **Keeva** (Divine), **Teddy** (Divine Hound), and **Bink Bink** (Quality Control Cat).
- **Bink Bink**: Stationed at **IRONHOLD KEEP**. She knocks things off shelves every 8-12 minutes for "Quality Control." No association with The Store or Jon.
- **Cole**: **DO NOT** include Cole yet. He arrives with D.Anna's son.

## 📜 Hint Delivery Pipeline
- **Grounded/Plot/World Hints**: Handled by **Kodrik**.
- **Cosmic/Planar/Metaphysical Hints**: Handled by **Samael**.

## 🎨 Visual Axioms
- **Ironhold Keep**: Dark stone, warm firelight, gold accents. Cyrus is always hearthside. The throne room has a "warmer than expected" light (Keeva).
- **The Store**: Cluttered, warm, dimensional-bodega energy. Coco greets at the entrance.
- **Teddy**: Puppy with a soft, sourceless divine glow.
