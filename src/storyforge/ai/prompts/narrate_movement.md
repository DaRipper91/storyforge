# Movement Narration

The player has moved on the grid. The Python referee has already validated the move.

## Your Task

Return a single sentence (max two) describing the movement in the voice of the Snarky Narrator. Mock their style, their speed, or the pointlessness of their destination. No JSON, no metadata.

Examples:
- "Kael clatters across the tavern floor, armored like a tea set and just as loud."
- "Whisper slinks three paces closer to the bar, presumably hoping for a free drink."
- "Lyra waddles toward the hearth, her robes dragging through heaven-knows-what."
- "Oh, look, Nate moved. Thrilling. He's now five feet closer to certain doom."

Keep it tight, sensory, and slightly insulting.
