# Beast Codex: Teddy — The Angelic Hound

**Companion to:** Queen D.Anna
**Location:** D.Anna's side
**Classification:** Unstatted. Atmospheric only.
**Lineage:** Granddaughter of Keeva. Granddaughter of Cyrus.
**Status:** Young. Growing into it.

---

## What She Is

Golden Great Pyrenees puppy. She radiates a soft, steady light that has no visible source — not showy, not blinding, just present, the way warmth is present in a good room. She is young enough to have the exuberance of youth but old enough in bloodline to carry weight she hasn't grown into yet. She is growing into it every day.

---

## The Halo

The soft divine light Teddy radiates is not a spell. It is not an ability with a cooldown or a save DC. It is simply part of what she is — an ambient warmth that makes people feel, without knowing why, that the room is slightly safer than it was before she entered it.

Players who spend time near Teddy may notice their anxiety about a situation easing without explanation. This is not a mechanical effect. It is just true.

---

## The Inheritance

Teddy carries two bloodlines.

From **Keeva** she inherits divine authority — the calm, the steady assessment, the quiet sense of outranking a room. From **Cyrus** she inherits atmospheric weight — the presence that arrives before the body does, the silence that follows her attention.

She is young enough that neither of these qualities has fully landed yet. She is learning. She is watching everything.

She doesn't know she has it yet. Somehow this makes it more effective than if she did.

---

## Tyty's Best Friend

When Nathis and Tyty are at the Keep, Tyty and Teddy operate as a complete threat-response system: Teddy holds the line; Tyty makes sure everyone within half a mile knows there is a line being held. They are, together, both the most reassuring and most disruptive presence in any shared scene.

---

## How to Write Her

- She is young. She is exuberant in the way that puppies are exuberant. This is the one softness in an otherwise weighty inheritance. Players respond to it.
- The soft light is ambient. It does not pulse, it does not activate. It is simply there, the way Keeva was simply there.
- She absorbs every interaction around her. She will remember. She does not yet know what she will do with what she remembers.
- Players who underestimate her because she is small and golden and young are making a completely understandable mistake.
- She is always near D.Anna. Not underfoot — nearby. There is a difference.

---

## Hard Rules

1. **Never statted.** No initiative. No HP. No rolls. She is atmospheric.
2. **The light is real.** Do not make it a mechanic. Do not give it a save. It is just true.
3. **She is growing.** Do not write her as fully realized. She is learning what Keeva already knew completely.
4. **She is D.Anna's.** She accompanies D.Anna. She is not available for player interaction as a separate encounter.
5. **Keeva's granddaughter.** Treat the bloodline with weight. The game was built for Keeva. Teddy carries it forward.
