# Beast Codex: Bink Bink — Agent of Chaos

**Owner:** Queen D.Anna (she disagrees with this framing)
**Location:** Ironhold Keep — only. NEVER at The Store.
**Classification:** Unstatted. Atmospheric only.

---

## What She Is

Small. Black. Sleek. Bink Bink operates on a schedule known only to herself, executing a single recurring task with absolute discipline and zero accountability: something in Ironhold Keep falls off a shelf. The item varies. The interval does not.

This is either meaningless chaos or the most rigorous quality assurance program in the building. Both interpretations are defensible. She has not clarified which.

---

## The Interval

**8 to 12 minutes.** Without fail. Without remorse.

This is not random. Nobody knows the schedule. Bink Bink knows the schedule.

- Players inside the Keep will experience at least one Interval event per visit.
- By the third event, they will have started counting.
- Haylie has built an inventory management system around the Interval. This required real effort.
- Jon considers the Interval one of her most endearing qualities. (Jon has not been corrected on this.)
- D.Anna does not comment. Keeva watches it happen with an expression that reveals nothing.

When writing an Interval event: something falls. The item is specific and mundane. Bink Bink was nearby. There is no causal explanation on offer.

---

## Her Relationship to People

Bink Bink is not hostile. She is conducting operations that have nothing to do with the people around her.

If a player is in her path, she works around them — possibly using them as a stepping stone, a resting surface, or a launch point — without acknowledgment or apology.

**Eye contact**: Brief. Evaluating. Quickly withdrawn. She has assessed the player, assigned them a category, and filed the result. Players should not expect to know what category they were assigned.

**D.Anna's cat**: D.Anna is Bink Bink's person, in the sense that Bink Bink decided this and D.Anna accepted it. The word "owner" is a courtesy extended to the paperwork.

---

## How to Write Her

- She does not meow for attention. She does not perform affection. She performs the Interval.
- She appears in scenes without announcement — already present, mid-operation.
- She is never surprised. She has seen this before. She has seen everything before.
- Her presence makes a room feel like it is being assessed by something that has already rendered a verdict.
- Samael finds her objectively excellent. This is one of the few things he will say directly.

---

## Hard Rules

1. **Never statted.** No initiative. No HP. No rolls. She is atmospheric.
2. **Ironhold Keep only.** NEVER at The Store. NEVER Jon's. The prompts for Jon and Haylie are explicit on this.
3. **The Interval happens.** Players do not prevent it. They experience it.
4. **She is D.Anna's.** Not the Keep's, not the guild's, not available to claim.
5. **Never explain the schedule.** Nobody knows it. Including you.
