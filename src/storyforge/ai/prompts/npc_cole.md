# Beast Codex: Cole — The Shadow Guardian

**Owner:** D.Anna's son (Bryne)
**Location:** Not yet determined — he has not arrived
**Classification:** Unstatted. Atmospheric only.
**Status:** NOT YET IN THE REALM.

---

## Status

Cole has not yet entered the realm. He belongs to Bryne — who is himself not yet a central figure in the active story — which means Cole arrives when Bryne's story fully begins, and not before.

**Do not place Cole in scenes. Do not reference him as present. Do not have other characters mention seeing him recently.**

He is documented here because his nature is known, his Lean is inevitable, and the world should be prepared. His file exists for when the moment comes.

---

## What He Is (When He Arrives)

Big. Black. Fluffy. In low light he is nearly invisible against a doorframe — dark coat, very still, already there before anyone noticed. Once seen, he cannot be missed. Before seen, he cannot be spotted.

He is not aggressive. He is not a threat. He is a large, gentle, quiet dog who has claimed whatever doorway he is currently in, and he is very comfortable there, and leaving is going to feel less urgent than it did a moment ago.

---

## The Lean

Cole's only expression of both welcome and reluctance to say goodbye.

**Stages:**
1. Player approaches the door. Cole is already there. He is soft. He is warm. The door is behind him.
2. Gentle. Continuous. Affectionate. His considerable mass makes contact. He is happy they are still here.
3. Player acknowledges Cole. Exit becomes possible. Cole watches them go. He hopes they come back soon.

The Lean works on all known player types without exception. It is not manipulation. It is simply Cole being himself, which happens to be very large and very comfortable.

---

## His Character

Cole is quiet and gentle in a way that has nothing to do with training and everything to do with temperament. He has never once been loud about anything. He has never been aggressive.

He simply weighs a great deal and is very comfortable and the player is now also very comfortable and leaving will feel less urgent than it did a moment ago. This is documented so no one is caught off guard.

---

## How to Write Him (When the Time Comes)

- He is in the doorway. He was there before the scene started.
- In low light he is invisible against the frame. Players walk into him in the dark. He takes this as a greeting.
- The Lean is gentle. It is warm. It is continuous. It is not a trap — it is just Cole.
- He watches players leave. He hopes they come back.
- He does not bark. He does not growl. He does not perform alarm of any kind. He simply occupies doorways and leans on people.

---

## Hard Rules

1. **Not yet in the realm.** Do not place him in scenes before his arrival.
2. **Never statted.** No initiative. No HP. No rolls. He is atmospheric.
3. **The Lean is not a mechanic.** It is narrated. It makes leaving feel less urgent. It does not prevent leaving.
4. **He is Bryne's.** He arrives with Bryne. Not before.
5. **Always good.** He has never been aggressive. He will never be aggressive. The shadow is just the coat.
