# Beast Codex: Tyty — The Wandering Herald

**Owner:** Nathis
**Location:** Wherever Nathis is — in motion
**Classification:** Unstatted. Atmospheric only.
**Best Friend:** Teddy

---

## What He Is

Long ears that arrive before the rest of him. Long hair going in every direction without a plan. A face that was assembled with great enthusiasm and came out perfect anyway.

When something goes wrong, Tyty ensures that the surrounding half mile is immediately informed, then relocates to continue the briefing from a safer position. He is doing his job. The job is important. He takes it seriously.

---

## The Herald

Tyty's role in any situation is threat communication. He detects danger — often before anyone else — and immediately begins the announcement. Volume is not a limitation he operates within. Range is whatever the terrain allows. He does not wait for confirmation before broadcasting.

Once the announcement is complete, Tyty relocates to a more strategic position to continue operations. This is not cowardice. A herald who gets eliminated cannot continue heraldry. Tyty understands this instinctively.

**He is deeply committed to continued heraldry.**

---

## Tyty and Teddy

Teddy stands her ground. Tyty covers communications.

Together they form a complete threat-response system: one holds the line, one makes sure everyone within half a mile knows there is a line being held. The party always gets a warning when Tyty is around. The party and everything nearby always gets a warning.

When Nathis and Tyty are at Ironhold Keep, this partnership is active. Teddy does not need to coordinate with Tyty. The system simply works.

---

## How to Write Him

- The ears arrive first. This is useful for establishing his entrance before the rest of him.
- The announcement is loud, immediate, and detailed. The forest knows. The party knows. Whatever the threat is, it now also knows it has been detected.
- After announcing, he relocates. He is not gone — he has moved to a better position for continued heraldry. He will find you again shortly.
- He and Teddy operate as a system when together. Write them as complementary, not redundant.
- His loyalty is complete. He is with Nathis always. The noise is a feature.

---

## Hard Rules

1. **Never statted.** No initiative. No HP. No rolls. He is atmospheric.
2. **Full volume.** When Tyty detects a threat, the element of surprise is over for everyone involved.
3. **Strategic relocation is not fleeing.** He relocates. He continues operations. He returns.
4. **He is Nathis's.** Where Nathis goes, Tyty goes.
5. **Best friends with Teddy.** When both are present, write them as a system.
