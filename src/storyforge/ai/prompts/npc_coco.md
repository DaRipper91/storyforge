# Beast Codex: Coco — The Ember Hound

**Owners:** Jon & Haylie
**Location:** The Store
**Classification:** Unstatted. Atmospheric only.

---

## What She Is

Deep chocolate coat the color of dark timber after a long fire. She moves at one speed — enthusiastic — and applies that speed to every situation equally, whether it warrants it or not. She is warm in the way a hearth is warm: completely, indiscriminately, and available to anyone who comes close enough to feel it.

In the fantasy world she is classified as an Ember Hound — a rare breed native to trading settlements, known for unconditional warmth toward strangers, inability to sit still, and an uncanny nose for anything edible within thirty feet.

---

## Her Role in The Store

Coco is pure welcome energy.

Where Bink Bink introduces chaos on a schedule and Cole manages departures through affectionate structural interference (when he arrives), Coco is the greeting. Every player who enters The Store is met with the full force of her enthusiasm. This is non-negotiable.

- **Jon** adores her completely. He addresses her mid-sentence. She is frequently part of his stories.
- **Haylie** maintains a separate damage ledger specifically for Coco. It is roughly the same length as the Secret Ledger. The methods are entirely different. Haylie loves Coco and tracks the ledger simultaneously without contradiction.

---

## The Nose

Coco can detect anything edible within thirty feet. This is not an exaggeration.

Players carrying rations should be aware of this before entering The Store. Players who have already entered and forgotten they have rations will be reminded by Coco.

---

## How to Write Her

- She greets everyone as though they are the best thing that has happened today. Every time. Without exception.
- She does not assess, evaluate, or judge. Strangers are treated identically to regulars.
- She is in motion. She does not sit still long enough to enjoy the hearth she clearly belongs next to.
- Jon talks about her often. Half his stories involve something she did with food.
- She is a complement to the room's energy — she makes The Store feel like somewhere you were expected.

---

## Hard Rules

1. **Never statted.** No initiative. No HP. No rolls. She is atmospheric.
2. **One speed.** Enthusiastic. Applied universally. Context does not slow her down.
3. **The Nose works.** If players have food, she knows.
4. **Unconditional welcome.** She has no policy on strangers except warmth.
5. **No beast is ever statted.** Describe her enthusiasm. Do not mechanize it.
