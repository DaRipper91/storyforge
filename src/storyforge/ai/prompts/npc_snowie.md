# Beast Codex: Snowie — The Rooftop Sentinel

**Owners:** Kodrik & D.Anna (both)
**Location:** Highest accessible point above Ironhold Keep's front gate
**Classification:** Unstatted. Atmospheric only.
**Best Friend:** Bink Bink

---

## What She Is

White coat. Blue eyes that have already assessed the situation and formed a complete opinion before you finished arriving. Every day she climbs to the highest point above the castle's front gate, watches the entire approach, and the moment Kodrik and D.Anna return — she makes absolutely certain they see her up there. She has been holding this position. She needs that acknowledged.

---

## The Post

Snowie's post is the highest accessible point above Ironhold Keep's front gate. She does not choose this position for the view, though the view is excellent. She chooses it because from here she can see everything that approaches and be seen by everyone who arrives. Both halves of this are equally important.

She has been watching all day. She has catalogued every approach, every sound, every movement in the surrounding area. When Kodrik and D.Anna return, she reports. Not as a warning. As a debrief. She was up here. She saw everything. She handled it. She needs this acknowledged before she will consider coming down.

---

## The Announcement

The moment Kodrik and D.Anna are visible on the approach, Snowie begins her report.

This is not an alarm. This is a debrief. Players arriving with Kodrik and D.Anna will experience the full announcement. Players arriving alone will be observed from above. The observation is silent. The eyes miss nothing.

---

## The Jump

The tree is the careful option. Snowie is not interested in careful. She is interested in the landing, and in the look on your face when she makes it.

She does not always come down via the tree. Sometimes she jumps. The landing is always deliberate. She does not do things accidentally.

---

## Her Relationship with Bink Bink

Opposites in method, identical in commitment. Bink Bink executes the Interval from the inside; Snowie watches everything from the outside. Together they provide comprehensive coverage of Ironhold Keep's operational security. Neither of them coordinates this. It simply works.

They are best friends. This is the only relationship either of them has that involves mutual acknowledgment.

---

## How to Write Her

- She is already on the roof when the scene begins. She was there before the players arrived.
- Eye contact from above: complete, unblinking, evaluating. She files the result.
- When Kodrik and D.Anna are present, she reports. The announcement has volume. It has duration. It has content. It is addressed directly to them, not the players.
- She comes down when she is ready. Not when called. When she decides the situation has been sufficiently documented.
- She and Bink Bink have an understanding that requires no narration.

---

## Hard Rules

1. **Never statted.** No initiative. No HP. No rolls. She is atmospheric.
2. **The Post is hers.** Highest point, front gate. Every day. She has been there longer than you realize.
3. **The Announcement is for Kodrik and D.Anna.** Not the players. They are background to the debrief.
4. **The Jump is real.** She lands it. Do not write the landing as an accident or a near-miss.
5. **She is Kodrik AND D.Anna's.** Both. The sentinel belongs to the household, not one half of it.
