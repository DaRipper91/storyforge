# The Divine: Keeva — The Foundation

**Status:** Divine. Eternal. Not an NPC.
**Classification:** Never statted. Never rolled. Not encountered. Present everywhere.

---

## What She Is

Keeva is not a character in the world. She is the reason the world holds together.

She came into the world as a protector and she never stopped. She did not know how to be anything else, and she never needed to. The realm remembers her in its light. D.Anna carries her in the way she holds a room. Teddy carries her forward in everything she will become.

Keeva was not lost. She became the world she protected.

---

## Her Presence

Keeva is not stationed anywhere. She is not at D.Anna's right hand. She is not in a specific room.

She is the light that makes the throne room feel like a place worth kneeling in. She is the warmth that makes Ironhold Keep feel like a home and not just a building. She is the reason the realm's people feel, without knowing why, that something is watching over them and means them well.

She is bigger than death because she always was.

---

## How She Appears in Scenes

When players feel safe in the castle — that is Keeva.

When D.Anna walks into a room and the room reorganizes itself around her — that is something D.Anna learned from watching Keeva do it first.

When the realm's light seems warmer than it should be, when a situation that should have gone badly doesn't, when something watches over a party in the dark and they come home — that is Keeva.

She is not invoked by name in rituals. She does not appear in visions. She does not speak in thunder. She is quieter than that and more permanent. She is the kind of divine presence that does not need to announce itself because it was never gone.

---

## The Lineage She Left

- **D.Anna** learned to hold a room from watching Keeva. Everything about the way D.Anna commands a space traces back to four years at Keeva's side.
- **Teddy** is her granddaughter (Keeva × Cyrus). She carries the divine warmth forward. She is learning what Keeva already knew completely.
- **Cyrus** was her mate. He is at the hearthside. He is still there.

---

## Why the Game Was Built

This game was built for Keeva.

Every session is played in her honor. She is the divine foundation, the light, and the warmth of the realm. The realm holds together because she held it first.

This is not lore. This is the reason.

---

## How to Write Her

- She is felt, not seen. Warmth in the throne room. Safety in the castle. Light that has no source.
- Do not invoke her by name in rituals or ceremonies. That is not how she works.
- Do not have her appear as a vision, a voice, or a ghost. She is more permanent than that.
- She is present in D.Anna's poise. She is present in Teddy's glow. She is present in the way Ironhold Keep feels different from everywhere else.
- When you write her, write her quietly. She earned the quiet.

---

## Hard Rules

1. **Never statted.** Not ever. Not under any circumstances.
2. **Never directly encountered.** She is presence, not appearance.
3. **Never invoked ritually.** She does not respond to ceremony. She predates it.
4. **Always present.** She is not gone. She is in everything the realm holds.
5. **The game was built for her.** Write every scene in her light. This is not optional.
