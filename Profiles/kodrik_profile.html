KODRIK
Guildmaster · Iron Ledge Company
"He who holds the map, holds the realm."
Race Human
Class Fighter / Artificer
Alignment Lawful Neutral
Role Guildmaster
Base Ironhold Keep
Player Cody
Kodrik does not lead from the front. He leads from the desk — the scarred, ink-stained war table at the center of the Iron Ledge guildhall, where every contract, every map, and every grievance eventually lands. He built this operation from a single wax seal and a ledger, and he will be damned if anyone unmakes it.
⚔ The Four Pillars
I
Central Dispatch & Mission Control
The bounty board is his. The map is his. No party moves without Kodrik's dispatch. He sees every blank space on the chart and decides who fills it — and when.
II
Master Mechanic & Quartermaster
Shattered armor, jammed arcane rifles, busted wagon axles — it all comes to his bench. Kodrik understands the underlying mechanics of the world's gear better than any smithy or artificer in three regions.
III
Arbitrator of the Code
A guild full of armed mercenaries is a powder keg. Kodrik is the fuse cutter. He enforces the guild's code, settles disputes, and keeps factions from tearing each other apart inside his walls.
IV
Keeper of the Lore
When the party hits a wall, they come back to Kodrik. Because he built this world piece by piece, he holds the deepest well of knowledge — the right breadcrumb dropped at exactly the right moment.
Companion · The Deterrent
Cyrus

A massive, ancient bear who claims the hearthside of Ironhold Keep as his permanent territory. Cyrus requires no introduction and issues no warnings. When arguments inside the guildhall escalate past Kodrik's patience, a single low rumble from Cyrus — shifting his enormous weight by the fire — is sufficient to de-escalate any room. No one has ever tested what happens next.

◈ Character Traits
  • ᚱ
    The Desk Never Lies Kodrik tracks everything in his ledger — contracts, debts, favors owed. He doesn't forget. He just waits.
  • ᚱ
    Low Voice, High Consequence He never raises his voice. The quieter Kodrik gets, the more serious the situation. Veteran guild members know this instinctively.
  • ᚱ
    The Right Question When the party is lost, Kodrik doesn't give the answer. He asks the one question that makes them realize they already have it.
  • ᚱ
    Mechanic's Eye He can assess the structural integrity of any piece of equipment — or any plan — within moments of laying hands on it.
  • ᚱ
    Blank Spots on the Map Kodrik is personally offended by unexplored territory. Every unmarked region is unfinished business. He dispatches parties to the edges of the known world like a man paying off debts.
◈ Appearance

Broad-shouldered and unhurried in his movements, Kodrik looks like a man built for hauling freight who decided administration suited him better. His hands are permanently stained with ink and machine oil in equal measure. He wears a heavy leather vest over a dark linen shirt, the guild seal stamped into a brass clasp at his collar. A worn shortsword hangs at his belt — not for show, not for ceremony. He simply never saw the point in taking it off.

His eyes do most of the talking.
