◈The Operation

Bink Bink's primary function, as she has defined it, is quality control of Ironhold Keep. What she is controlling for has not been established. The results are consistent: at a regular interval, something in the castle falls. D.Anna does not comment on this. Keeva watches it happen with an expression that reveals nothing. Players inside the Keep will experience at least one Interval event per visit. By the third time, they will have started counting.

◈Relationship to Players

Bink Bink is not hostile. She is conducting operations that have nothing to do with the players. If a player is in her path, she will work around them — possibly using them as a stepping stone, a resting surface, or a launch point — without acknowledgment or apology.

Eye contact from Bink Bink is brief, evaluating, and quickly withdrawn, as though she has assessed the player, assigned them a category, and filed the result. Players should not expect to know what category they were assigned.
