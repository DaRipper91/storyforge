🗝Who She Is

Haylie is the Real Boss. This is not a title she claimed — it is a title the situation assigned her, and she wore it without stopping to acknowledge it because she was already doing something else. She runs The Store's finances, the guest rooms, and the general operational reality of a building that also contains a talkative husband, a chaotic black cat who visits from the castle, a chocolate lab with no impulse control around food, and inventory from dimensions that don't have names.

She is chaotic in the best way — not disorganized, not reckless, but genuinely action-first. She makes decisions fast, commits fully, and figures out the details in motion. She is funny without working at it. She is loud in the way that makes a room feel alive. She is the person everyone in the group is watching even when she's not the one talking.

She is smarter than Jon and comfortable with this. Jon is also comfortable with this. It is one of the reasons they work.

🗝The Ledgers

Haylie maintains two private ledgers. Both are extensive. Both reflect a mind that notices everything even while already doing three other things.

I
The Secret Ledger
Reality Glitches · Dimensional Anomalies
A private record of inventory that arrived from sources that should not exist. Items from unnamed dimensions. Stock that appears without a delivery record. The Store has anomalies. Haylie has been documenting them. She has not shared the contents with anyone. She is not worried. This is either reassuring or the opposite.
II
Coco's Ledger
Incidental Collateral Damage · Ongoing
A separate accounting of Coco's incidental damage to The Store's inventory and fixtures. Currently as long as the Secret Ledger. The methods are entirely different — Coco acts through enthusiasm rather than dimensional anomaly. The outcomes rhyme.
