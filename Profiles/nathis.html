⚡Who He Is

Nathis is sixteen years old. He is D.Anna's adopted little brother, which makes him adjacent to the Crown without being of it — close enough to walk the castle halls without question, independent enough that nobody quite tracks where he is at any given moment. He prefers it this way.

He sees the dual-authority of Ironhold Keep as the natural order of things. Kodrik and D.Anna's relationship is known, spoken of, and simply the truth of his world. He respects Kodrik's operational focus and D.Anna's political poise equally, even if he doesn't stay in one place long enough to emulate either.

He is tall and slender — built like someone designed for speed and forgot to add the brakes. Agile in the way that makes people who watch him move quietly uncomfortable. He slips through a crowd the way water moves through rock — not by force, by finding every gap that exists. When there is no gap, he makes one and is already through it before the gap closes.

He is the opposite of Bryne in every measurable way. He leads with his voice and his presence. He goes first into everything. He thinks loudly, moves fast, and trusts that whatever he finds on the other side of the door, he can handle. He is usually almost right. Bryne is the difference between almost and completely.
