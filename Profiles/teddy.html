Golden Great Pyrenees puppy. She radiates a soft, steady light that has no visible source — not showy, not blinding, just present, the way warmth is present in a good room. She is young enough to have the exuberance of youth but old enough in bloodline to carry weight she hasn't grown into yet. She is growing into it every day.
☀The Halo

The soft divine light Teddy radiates is not a spell. It is not an ability with a cooldown or a save DC. It is simply part of what she is — an ambient warmth that makes people feel, without knowing why, that the room is slightly safer than it was before she entered it.

Players who spend time near Teddy may notice their anxiety about a situation easing without explanation. This is not a mechanical effect. It is just true.

☀The Inheritance

Teddy carries two bloodlines. From her grandmother Keeva she inherits divine authority — the calm, the steady assessment, the quiet sense of outranking a room. From her grandfather Cyrus she inherits atmospheric weight — the presence that arrives before the body does, the silence that follows her attention.

She is young enough that neither of these qualities has fully landed yet. She is learning. She is watching everything. Players who underestimate her because she is small and golden and young are making a completely understandable mistake that they will eventually recognize as such.
