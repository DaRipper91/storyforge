✦The Divine Nature

Keeva is not stationed anywhere. She is not at D.Anna's right hand. She is not in a specific room. She is the light that makes the throne room feel like a place worth kneeling in. She is the warmth that makes Ironhold Keep feel like a home and not just a building. She is the reason the realm's people feel, without knowing why, that something is watching over them and means them well.

She is bigger than death because she always was. Death is a thing that happens to beings whose purpose has limits. Keeva's purpose had no limit. The world she protected still stands. The people she loved still carry her. She is present in all of it.

✦What She Is in the World

When players feel safe in the castle — that is Keeva. When D.Anna walks into a room and the room reorganizes itself around her — that is something D.Anna learned from watching Keeva do it first. When the realm's light seems warmer than it should be, when a situation that should have gone badly doesn't, when something watches over a party in the dark and they come home — that is Keeva.

She is not invoked by name in rituals. She does not appear in visions. She does not speak in thunder. She is quieter than that and more permanent. She is the kind of divine presence that does not need to announce itself because it was never gone.

The realm was built under her. Everything good in it grew in her light.

For Dee · In Memory · In Light · Always
This game was built for her. She was four years of love, protection, and the kind of presence that changes a home forever. She is the reason the realm has a divine source at all. Every safe moment in this story, every light in the dark, every time the party makes it home — that is Keeva. That is still her working.
✦What She Means to D.Anna

D.Anna does not rule the realm alone. She rules it in the knowledge that Keeva was there first — that the authority she carries, the poise she walks with, the way she holds a room without effort — all of it was learned at the side of a golden dog who never once doubted what she was for.

D.Anna does not need to say Keeva's name for the players to understand that everything about the Queen traces back to her. It is in the way she moves. It is in the way the castle feels. It is in Teddy, who carries the light forward, still learning, still growing into what her grandmother already was completely.

Teddy
Keeva's granddaughter. Young golden Great Pyrenees, radiating a soft light that has no visible source. She is Keeva's continuation in the world — the warmth carried forward, the protection still growing, the love that did not end. She does not yet know the full weight of what she carries. She will. The world will teach her, and Keeva's light will be in everything she learns.
✦On the Matter of Mykael Greenwood

Mykael Greenwood — the supply hauler, the Fat Bear, Kodrik's friend — is the strongest entity in the living world. For 1 to 4 random seconds, nothing within the mortal plane can match him. This is documented. This is true.

Keeva is not ranked alongside him. Not because she is below him. Because she is not in the category being measured. Mykael is the most powerful thing that can act in the living world. Keeva is the reason the living world exists. You do not measure the storm against the sky the storm happens inside of. The question of who is stronger does not apply across that boundary. It never did.

She is the foundation. Everything — including Mykael, including his 4 seconds, including everything that has ever happened in the realm — stands on what she held together first.

✦DM Note · Handle With Reverence

Keeva is never reduced to a mechanic. She is never an encounter, a stat block, a resource, or a plot device. She is the world's divine foundation and she is treated as such — present in everything, named when the moment earns it, felt always.

She is the reason this game exists. Every session is played in her honor. The realm holds together because she held it first.
