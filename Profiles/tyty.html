📣The Herald

Tyty's role in any situation is threat communication. He detects danger — often before anyone else — and immediately begins the announcement. Volume is not a limitation he operates within. Range is whatever the terrain allows. He does not wait for confirmation before broadcasting. Speed of information is the priority.

Once the announcement is complete, Tyty relocates to a more strategic position to continue operations. This is not cowardice. A herald who gets eliminated cannot continue heraldry. Tyty understands this instinctively. He is deeply committed to continued heraldry.

Teddy stands her ground. Tyty covers the communications. Together they form a complete threat-response system — one holds the line, one makes sure everyone within half a mile knows there is a line being held. The party always gets a warning when Tyty is around. The party and everything nearby always gets a warning.
