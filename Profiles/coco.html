🔥The Ember Hound

In the fantasy world Coco is classified as an Ember Hound — a rare breed native to trading settlements and waystation inns, known for their unconditional warmth toward strangers, their inability to sit still, and their uncanny nose for anything edible within a thirty-foot radius.

Ember Hounds do not guard. They do not deter. They welcome, relentlessly and without discrimination, which in a store that also contains Bink Bink's Interval and Cole's eventual Lean, provides important emotional counterbalance.

🔥Role in The Store

Where Bink Bink introduces chaos on a schedule and Cole manages departures through affectionate structural interference, Coco is pure welcome energy. She greets every player who enters The Store with the full force of her enthusiasm, which is considerable and non-negotiable.

Jon adores her. Haylie tracks the number of things Coco has knocked over, which is a separate ledger from the one she keeps for Bink Bink. The two ledgers are roughly equal in length. The methods are entirely different.

Owner & Family

Owner & Manager
Madame Haylie
Haylie loves Coco and maintains the second ledger. Both things are true simultaneously. She has developed a system for keeping Coco away from the inventory shelves at peak hours. The system works approximately seventy percent of the time.
🔥The Store Animal Hierarchy

The Store operates with three animal presences, each occupying a distinct niche. Bink Bink holds interior chaos operations on a precise schedule. Coco holds the welcome and ambient warmth of the space, along with secondary chaos as a byproduct of enthusiasm. Cole will eventually hold the door, when D.Anna's son arrives to bring him.

Together they form a complete ecosystem. Haylie keeps records on all of them. Jon loves all of them equally and unconditionally, which is both his greatest quality and the primary source of the second ledger.
