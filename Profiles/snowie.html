🏔The Sentinel

Snowie's post is the highest accessible point above Ironhold Keep's front gate. She does not choose this position for the view, though the view is excellent. She chooses it because it is the position from which she can see everything that approaches and be seen by everyone who arrives. Both halves of this are equally important to her.

She has been watching all day. She has catalogued every approach, every sound, every movement in the surrounding area. When Kodrik and D.Anna return, she announces this. Not as a warning. As a report. She was up here. She saw everything. She handled it. She needs this acknowledged before she will consider coming down.

She does not always climb down. Sometimes she jumps. The tree is the careful option. Snowie is not interested in careful. She is interested in the landing, and in the look on your face when she makes it.

    A Fighter Like Bink Bink, Snowie is a fighter. Unlike Bink Bink, there is no schedule and no subtlety. What needs handling gets handled. She does not sneak up on situations. She arrives visibly, at height, and with full commitment.
On the Matter of the Jump
The tree is there. She knows the tree is there. She has used the tree. On the days she chooses the jump instead — and there are many such days — she makes the calculation, commits fully, and lands. Then she looks at you. Then she walks away. The tree was an option. The jump was a choice. There is a difference and she knows it and she needs you to know that she knows it.
