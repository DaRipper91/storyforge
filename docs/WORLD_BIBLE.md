# 📖 StoryForge — World Bible: THE FERAL WORLD

**Era**: IRON LEDGE ERA

---

## 🌎 Section 1 — The World
The world operates on a dual-power structure centered at **IRONHOLD KEEP**:
- **QUEEN D.ANNA**: Political authority as Head of State.
- **GUILDMASTER KODRIK**: Operational authority through the Iron Ledge Company.

This arrangement is stable because both understand their respective domains.
**History**: The world is divided into the **BEFORE ERA** and the **AFTER ERA**. Some beings have transformed from mortal forms into larger entities; the mechanism is known lore but the process is not to be invented by agents.

---

## 🕊️ Section 2 — The Divine: KEEVA
**Keeva** holds a category above all others. She is not an NPC, guardian, or mechanic. She is the **divine source of the realm**.
- **Meaning**: "Beloved" and "Protector."
- **Status**: The realm's light, warmth, and protection. She is everywhere.
- **Presence**: Felt as safety in the castle and quiet authority in D.Anna. She is the reason the party makes it home through the dark.
- **Teddy**: Her granddaughter, a young golden Great Pyrenees, is the living continuation of Keeva's light.
- **Rule**: Keeva is NEVER a mechanic, encounter, or plot device. Name her only when the moment is earned. This game is built in her honor.
