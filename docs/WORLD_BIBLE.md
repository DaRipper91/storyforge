# 📖 StoryForge — World Bible: THE FERAL WORLD

**Era**: IRON LEDGE ERA

---

## 🌎 Section 1 — The World
The world operates on a dual-power structure centered at **IRONHOLD KEEP**:
- **QUEEN D.ANNA**: Political authority as Head of State.
- **GUILDMASTER KODRIK**: Operational authority through the Iron Ledge Company.

This arrangement is stable because both understand their respective domains.
**History**: The world is divided into the **BEFORE ERA** and the **AFTER ERA**. Some beings have transformed from mortal forms into larger entities; the mechanism is known lore but the process is not to be invented by agents.

---

## 🕊️ Section 2 — The Divine: KEEVA
**Keeva** holds a category above all others. She is not an NPC, guardian, or mechanic. She is the **divine source of the realm**.
- **Meaning**: "Beloved" and "Protector."
- **Status**: The realm's light, warmth, and protection. She is everywhere.
- **Presence**: Felt as safety in the castle and quiet authority in D.Anna. She is the reason the party makes it home through the dark.
- **Teddy**: Her granddaughter, a young golden Great Pyrenees, is the living continuation of Keeva's light.
- **Rule**: Keeva is NEVER a mechanic, encounter, or plot device. Name her only when the moment is earned. This game is built in her honor.

## 🐾 Section 4 — Beasts & Companions
**Critical Rule**: NEVER stat any beast. No initiative, HP, or combat roles.

### Ironhold Keep
- **KEEVA**: Divine source. Present as ambient warmth/light.
- **CYRUS (Winter Wolf)**: Kodrik's companion. Great Dane frame, Great Pyrenees coat, smoke-white. Keeva's mate. Teddy's grandfather. Permanent hearthside resident. Atmospheric deterrent — when he raises his head, the room de-escalates. Never announced in scene descriptions; simply present.
- **TEDDY (Divine Hound)**: D.Anna's side. Young golden Great Pyrenees puppy. Radiates a soft sourceless light. Living continuation of Keeva. Carries both Keeva's spiritual authority and Cyrus's atmospheric weight — does not yet know it.
- **BINK BINK (Black Cat)**: **Owner: QUEEN D.ANNA. Location: IRONHOLD KEEP.** Small, black, sleek. Performs quality control (knocking things off shelves) every 8–12 minutes. The interval is clockwork. It will not be explained.
- **SNOWIE (White Cat)**: **Owners: KODRIK & D.ANNA. Location: ABOVE THE FRONT GATE.** White coat, blue eyes. Posts at the highest point above the gate every day. Watches the entire approach. The moment Kodrik and D.Anna return, she announces it — not as alarm, as report. Sometimes comes down via the tree. Often jumps. Does not explain the jump. Best friend: Bink Bink. They are opposites: Snowie is exterior/visible/announces herself; Bink Bink is interior/shadow/scheduled. Together they cover the whole castle.
- **COLE (Shadow Hound)**: **Status: PENDING.** Big, black, fluffy. Belongs to Bryne (D.Anna's son). Arrives when Bryne arrives. Will find a door and claim it. Interaction method: The Lean — gentle, affectionate, continuous. Makes departing feel less urgent.

### The Store
- **COCO (Ember Hound)**: Jon/Haylie's companion. Deep chocolate coat. Moves at one speed: enthusiastic. Detects anything edible within 30ft. Haylie maintains a separate ledger for her collateral damage.

### The Field
- **TYTY (Wandering Herald)**: Nathis's dog. Long ears, long hair, goofy face assembled with great enthusiasm. When danger arrives, Tyty informs the surrounding half-mile at maximum volume, then relocates to continue the briefing from a safer position. This is not cowardice — a herald who gets eliminated cannot continue heraldry. Best friend: Teddy. Together: one holds the line, one tells the forest there is a line.
