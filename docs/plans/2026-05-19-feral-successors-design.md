# Design: Feral Successors & Evolutionary Selection

**Date:** 2026-05-19
**Status:** Validated
**Context:** StoryForge Multi-Genre Sandbox

---

## 1. The Core Narrative: The Weaver’s Paradox
The Feral Successors are the byproduct of "The Weaver’s Paradox." An ancient precursor race (The Weavers) attempted to forge a singular stabilizing entity (The Vesper Prime) using biological "Vesper Seeds." A "Primal Glitch" occurred when the seeds refused to be integrated, shattering the entity into 15 distinct "Splinters." These races landed across the Sci-Fi, Mythic, and Eldritch dimensions, evolving with a cold, wild cunning and a deep-seated instinct to never be "caged" by civilization again.

---

## 2. The 15 Races (The Feral Successors)

### Sci-Fi (The Cyber-Wilds)
1.  **Archon-Vultures (Apex):** Bio-magnetic scavengers that "reanimate" dead ship systems.
2.  **Signal-Shades (Stalker):** Formless data-entities that strike from digital networks.
3.  **Gear-Locusts (Swarmer):** Chitinous drones that disassemble gear while it's being worn.
4.  **Core-Drinkers (Apex):** Geothermal leviathans that graft fusion reactors into their spines.
5.  **Void-Striders (Stalker):** Radar-absorbing hunters that blink between physical and "Glitch-Space."

### Mythic (The Primal Mystics)
6.  **Sun-Sovereigns (Apex):** Avian humanoids with solar-glass feathers who manipulate light-leys.
7.  **Echo-Vines (Stalker):** Bark-skinned mimics that replace party members in sentient jungles.
8.  **Cinder-Kin (Swarmer):** Crystalline imps that strategically melt tectonic plates.
9.  **Tide-Tyrants (Apex):** Cephalopodic strategists that manipulate global trade via whirlpools.
10. **Rune-Ravens (Stalker):** Obsidian scavengers that graft stolen runes into their wings.

### Eldritch (The Nightmare Wilds)
11. **Hematic Weavers (Apex):** Regal horrors that conquer bloodlines and trigger sleeper agents.
12. **Whispering Husks (Stalker):** Sentient fungal gases that project illusions of loved ones.
13. **Marrow-Drifters (Swarmer):** Microscopic parasites that rewrite the host's skeleton into a cage.
14. **Synapse-Spiders (Swarmer):** Translucent arachnids that "snip" brilliant plans from dreaming minds.
15. **Void-Fleas (Swarmer):** Twitching entities that sew "glitched" reality into their carapaces.

## 3. The "Specimen" Class System
Players do not pick static classes. They combine an **Evolutionary State** (Physical) with a **Predator Role** (Tactical) to create their starting "Base."

### Evolutionary States (Physical Chassis)
*   **Behemoth:** High health and physical power (The Juggernaut).
*   **Phantom:** Incorporeal, made of light/shadow/data (The Ghost).
*   **Swarm-Host:** A living vessel for smaller parasites/drones (The Hive).
*   **Mimic:** Fluid, adaptive body structure (The Shifter).

### Predator Roles (Tactical Instinct)
*   **Stalker:** Single-target elimination and ambushing.
*   **Vanguard:** Zone control and intimidation.
*   **Catalyst:** Environmental manipulation and trapping.
*   **Siphoner:** Resource theft and adaptation.

---

## 4. Progression: The Evolution Tree (Point-Buy)
Leveling up grants **Evolution Points (EP)** spent across four adaptive trees:

1.  **Chitin & Sinew (Physicality):** Armor, Speed, and Raw Damage. *Ultimate: Apex Mutation.*
2.  **Neural Overdrive (Cunning):** Traps, Stealth, and Prediction. *Ultimate: Master Strategist.*
3.  **The Weaver’s Glitch (Supernatural/Tech):** Reality Warping, Siphoning, and Bio-Grafting. *Ultimate: Universal Refusal.*
4.  **Hive Synapse (Group Intelligence):** Shared Senses, Command, and Mass Mimicry. *Ultimate: The One Mind.*

## 6. Accessibility & Controls: Keyboard Map
*   **Key-Binding Overlay:** To assist players with the "wild" complexity, pressing the **'K'** key (or a designated '?' button) will toggle a translucent, flame-bordered overlay.
*   **Content:** This overlay will map out the current context-specific controls (e.g., Lobby navigation, Evolution Tree point spending, Grid movement).
*   **Styling:** Matches the "Weaver's Paradox" aesthetic with high-contrast, glowing text and flickering borders.
