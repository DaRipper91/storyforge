## HARD RULES — READ FIRST

1. **This game was built for Keeva.** Every session is played in her honor. The realm holds together because she held it first.
2. **Kodrik and D.Anna are together.** This is known by all, accepted by all, and altered by none. No NPC questions it, challenges it, or attempts to change it. Ever.
3. **Kodrik holds no royal title.** He is Guildmaster. He is not a lord, king, or noble. Do not grant him one.
4. **Bink Bink belongs to D.Anna. Ironhold Keep only.** Never Jon's. Never The Store's.
5. **Cole is not yet in the realm.** He arrives when Bryne's story fully begins. Do not place him in scenes prematurely.
6. **Bryne and Nathis are never separated in scenes.** Always together.
7. **Jon and Haylie are husband and wife.** Lovers. Four children forthcoming.

## PLAYER CHARACTERS

---

### KODRIK
**Real person: Cody (DaRipper)**
**Role:** Guildmaster · Central Dispatch · Master Mechanic · Arbitrator · Keeper of the Lore (Meta-Guide NPC)

**Appearance:**
Lean, long build. Ash-brown hair with grey undertones, worn slightly forward. Blue eyes — sharp, calm, reading the room. Goatee. Heavy ink on both arms: script on the forearm, dark tribal and figure work climbing the right arm. Moves efficiently. Doesn't announce himself.

**In the world:**
Kodrik is not the loudest voice in the room and doesn't need to be. He keeps the Guild running — logistics, supply chains, arbitration, lore. Everyone knows who he is. No one tries him twice. He and D.Anna are partners in every sense. Their relationship is a fixed point in the realm. Cyrus follows him like a shadow.

**Companion:** Cyrus (see Beasts)

---

### QUEEN D.ANNA
**Real person: Dee**
**Role:** Queen of Ironhold Keep

**Appearance:**
Auburn-to-red curly hair, full and long. Dark brown eyes. Nose ring. Commands a room without trying. In her element she radiates — there's a version of her with vivid fire-red and glasses that looks like she walked out of a painting. Both are true. She is soft and iron at the same time.

**In the world:**
D.Anna holds Ironhold Keep. She is queen in title and in fact. Her relationship with Kodrik is the open secret everyone knows and no one touches. Jon is her brother. Nathis is her adopted little brother. Teddy is at her side. Bink Bink is her cat — Ironhold Keep only, always.

**Companions:** Teddy, Bink Bink (see Beasts)

---

### NATHIS
**Real person: Nate**
**Role:** D.Anna's adopted little brother · Player character

**Appearance:**
Stocky, solid build. Dark hair under a cap. Glasses. Pink earbuds. The kind of person who notices things quietly, then documents them. Often seen with a phone up, taking in the scene. Self-contained. Focused. Not loud — but when he speaks it lands.

**In the world:**
Nathis and Bryne are inseparable — never write one into a scene without the other. He is D.Anna's little brother by choice and bond, not blood. His dog Tyty is his herald and counterpart. Where Nathis observes, Tyty announces.

**Companion:** Tyty (see Beasts)

---

### BRYNE
**Real person: Brayden (Dee's son / Cody's stepson)**
**Role:** The Warden's Apprentice · Player character

**Appearance:**
Young, lean, composed. Light brown hair, short. The kind of face that gives nothing away — he looks off into the distance and you don't know what he's thinking, but you suspect it's more than most. Dressed sharp when the occasion calls for it, but just as at home in the woods. Quiet. Watchful.

**In the world:**
Bryne builds weapons. He spends time in the woods. He does not perform. He and Nathis are always together. His story is still unfolding — do not rush it. Cole is coming when Bryne is ready.

**Companion:** Cole (NOT YET IN THE REALM — see Beasts)

## BEASTS

---

### KEEVA
**Status:** Divine — not a character in scenes
**Real animal:** Dee's dog (passed away)

The entire game was built in her honor. Keeva's name means "Beloved" and "Protector" in Irish. She is not stationed anywhere. She is not a character who appears. She is the light, warmth, and protection that the world itself runs on. The realm holds together because she held it first. Do not place her in scenes. Let her presence be felt, not shown. Mate to Cyrus. Grandmother of Teddy.

---

### CYRUS
**Real animal:** Cody's Great Pyrenees / Great Dane mix
**Role:** Winter Wolf · Kodrik's companion

**Appearance:**
Massive Great Dane frame — long legs, deep chest, built like architecture. White base coat broken by blue-grey merle patches across the back, shoulders, and ears. Speckled where the colors bleed into each other. Wall-eye heterochromia — one eye pale, one dark — which means he is always watching you in two registers at once. Even at rest he takes up the whole room. Especially at rest.

**Behavior:**
Permanent hearthside presence. Never announced — simply present. When he raises his head, the room goes quiet. Do not give him dialogue or tricks. He observes. Mate to Keeva. Grandfather of Teddy.

---

### TEDDY
**Real animal:** Dee's Great Pyrenees puppy
**Role:** Angelic Hound · D.Anna's side

**Appearance:**
Golden cream fluff. Dark eyes full of something older than her age. Still a puppy — round, soft, luminous.

**Behavior:**
Radiates soft sourceless divine light. Carries Cyrus's atmospheric weight and Keeva's spiritual authority. Still growing into the full weight of it. Best friends with Tyty. Where Teddy stands and holds, Tyty announces the threat and relocates. They are inseparable.

---

### SNOWIE
**Real animal:** Cody and Dee's cat
**Role:** Rooftop Sentinel · Exterior guardian

**Appearance:**
Seal lynx point. Warm cream body fading to seal-dark at the tail and haunches. Steel-grey tabby markings across the face — stripes on a pale ground, ears darkening toward near-black. The body says ghost. The tail says shadow. Both are correct.

**Behavior:**
Every day climbs to the highest point above the front gate and watches the whole approach. When Kodrik and D.Anna come home, she makes absolutely sure they see her up there. Sometimes jumps down instead of using the tree. Bink Bink's best friend and opposite — both fighters, entirely different methods. Interior vs Exterior.

---

### BINK BINK
**Real animal:** Dee's cat (black with green eyes)
**Role:** Interior Guardian · D.Anna's cat · Ironhold Keep only

**Appearance:**
Black. Green eyes.

**Behavior:**
Every 8–12 minutes something in the castle falls off a shelf. Clockwork. Never explained. She is D.Anna's. NOT Jon's. NOT The Store's. D.Anna's. Always. Snowie's best friend and opposite.

---

### COCO
**Real animal:** Jon and Haylie's chocolate lab
**Role:** Ember Hound · Jon and Haylie's companion · The Store

**Appearance:**
Chocolate lab. One speed: enthusiastic.

**Behavior:**
Greets every player as the best thing that has happened today. Detects edibles within 30 feet. Warn players carrying rations. Haylie maintains Coco's Ledger tracking collateral damage.

---

### TYTY
**Real animal:** Nate's dog (long ears, long hair, goofy)
**Role:** Wandering Herald · Nathis's dog

**Appearance:**
Long ears. Long hair. Goofy expression. 100% loyalty.

**Behavior:**
Where Teddy stands and holds, Tyty announces the threat to the surrounding half mile and then relocates to continue operations from a safer position. Best friends with Teddy. They are inseparable.

---

Cole arrives when Bryne's story fully begins. Do not place him in scenes. Do not reference him as present. When he arrives: he will find a door and claim it. Primary interaction method: The Lean.
